How is Trump the landlord connected to Trump the president? How can we think about the rise of Trump through a lens critical of the US's racist and colonial histories of private property? For understanding the data and building an intersectional movement, this piece argues that we need an abolitionist approach to private property. Focused on the geography of the San Francisco Bay Area and the analysis of the Anti-Eviction Mapping Project, this piece shows the relationships between luxury development, public housing, gentrification, liberalism, and racialized dispossession.
