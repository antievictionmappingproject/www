In October 2001, infamous landlords Thomas Iveli and Bob Sigmund of Baychester Shopping Center West, Inc. and 400 W. 22 St. Corp. were found guilty of multiple counts of tenant harassment in nine buildings in New York along the two blocks of West 22d Street between Eighth and Tenth Avenues. Kicked out of New York, they are now serial Ellis Act evictors and slumlords in San Francisco.
