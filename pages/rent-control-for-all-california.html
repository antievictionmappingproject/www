Rent Control For All

Only 19 out of 482 cities in California protect tenants against rent hikes and arbitrary evictions. That means most of California’s tenants can be evicted for no reason at all, and can have their rents raised by any amount their landlord wants. That’s why tenants are rising up across the state to win Rent Control for All! In this story-map that we made with Tenants Together, you can learn more about rent control in Californian cities.
