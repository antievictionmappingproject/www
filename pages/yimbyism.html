On YIMBYism

There have always been housing justice politics that defy the YIMBY dialectical worldview of NIMBY vs. YIMBY. The racial capitalism that laid the grounds for NIMBYism has mutated into that of YIMBYism. In other words, the racial exclusionary logics of NIMBYism have shifted through what Jodi Melamed describes as neoliberal multiculturalism, fomenting racist YIMBY proclamations that “all homes matter.”
