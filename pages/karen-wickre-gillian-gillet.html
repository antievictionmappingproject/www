Karen Wicrke is the Editorial Director at Twitter. Her Twitter profile describes her as "Word wrangler @ Twitter. Media obsessive, art & dog enthusiast. Internet lover. Amused by much. Friend of #newsfoo, @ICFJ, @Shebooks, TED, @Blogher." 

When hired in 2011, Wickre said her role at Twitter “will involve a fair amount of wordsmithing as well as nurturing a consistent Twitter voice across our public messages and information pages.” She had been at Google an epic nine years. This is the first official Google blog post, detailing a history of Google’s blogs as told by Wickre to Search Engine Land in 2007. 

According to the Noe Valley Voice, Wickre was named the "most connected woman in Silicon Valley."

Her blog is here: http://kvox.blogspot.com/

Gillian Gillett

Gillett served for a number of years as chief of staff to the current District 8 member of the Board of Supervisors Scott Wiener and now holds the position of Director of Transportation Policy to Mayor Ed Lee. She is married to Jeff Goldberg and they have two children. 

Before directing the Transportation Policy City and County of San Francisco, she worked as Chair of the Transportation Committee for SPUR. She also ran small software consulting company in the Mission.

She is part of the Mayors' Institute on City Design.

She claims to be a transit-oriented housing advocate.
