BONNIE K. SPINDLER

Bonnie K. Spindler is the "Victorian Specialist" at Zephyr Real Estate.

She has issued Ellis Act Eviction notices at the the following addresses:

  • 238 Divisadero Street (3 units) - Petition L121448, 9/12/2012; Petition L151201 on 6/5/2015; Petition L151201 on 6/11/2015 by Spindler Properties, LLC and Sally Jo Spindler. Two senior and/or disabled tenants received eviction notices there.

  • 215 7th Avenue (6 units) - Petition L071385, 9/12/2007 by 215-7th Avenue, LLC.

  • 1550 Fell Street (1 unit) - Petition L070581, 4/17/2007 by Bonnie Spindler, Trustee of the Bonnie Spindler Trust dated June 14, 2000.

  • 170-172 Fairmount Street (2 units) - Petition L030509, 4/15/2003 by Spindler Properties, LLC \ David Daniel Bowes.

  • 348-350 Scott - Spindler bought the property in May of 1998 for $430,000; in September, she gave tenants an Ellis eviction notice. By February, 2000, she had sold all 4 units for a total of $975,000.

She also abused OMI evictions in the Lower Haight in the late 1990s, including at 1550 Fell.

She has been an advocate for landlord rights, pushing a bill to prevent tenants from suing landlords. 

Her biography on Zephyr describes her early interest in “fixers” and real estate. According to her site, she purchased her first real estate investment at the age of 23, after which, she began “fixing and flipping” properties. She markets herself as a “Victorian specialist” who lives an 1893 Victorian built by Isadore Zellerbach. In addition to Victorians, Spindler sells Gothic, modern, TICs, new construction, and commercial real estate.  

 

 

Bonnie K. Spindler is the President of Spindler, Inc. She is also the Managing Member of Spindler Properties, LLC, and has been associated with 1360 - 62 Fulton, LLC, and 1625-1627 McAllister, LLC with Lena Lundberg and Diane Mulligan. She has held active roles in companies such as  Revision Tic Consulting, LLC, Revision Tic Consulting (Sf) with Gregory Fishman, LLC, and The Victorian Alliance. In the past, she was a member of Spindler Real Estate Associates, Ltd., 215-7th Avenue, LLC with Arnold Levinson, 1031-37 Divisadero, LLC, 1360 - 62 Fulton, LLC, Spindler Properties, LLC, and 350 Scott, LLC.
