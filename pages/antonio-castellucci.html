ANTONIO CASTELLUCCI

Antonio Castelucci and wife RitaCastelluccitake over a property and launch a variety of strategies to evict the tenants. Castellucci owns a number of smaller properties throughout San Francisco. Tenant activists say that, for the most part, he's a hands-off manager; however the management company he employs is fairly callous in dealing with the needs of tenants. When they acquire a building, if they discover that a tenant is a long-term resident, a common practice is to try to find some way to either intimidate the tenant out of the unit or to fabricate a just cause to vacate the unit.

Socialites, they live in Belvedere.They flip properties, and Ellis Evictions are another tool in their vast real estate portfolio.
